Transition Path Sampling
with Improved Off-Policy Training of Diffusion Path Samplers





Sampled transition paths by TPS-DPS

Here, we present the evaluation of our method in small real-world molecular system with various numbers of amino acids: Alanine Dipeptide, Polyproline Helix, and Chignolin. We present the initial and target state, and the transition sampled from TPS-DPS as video.
